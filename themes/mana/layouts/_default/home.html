❓ The fundamentals — explained

What exactly do I get?

A professional email address at your own domain (e.g. you@yourcompany.co.za). Webmail access, spam & virus protection, and the ability to send/receive from any device. Plus free SSL and DNS management.

Can I use my own domain?

Absolutely. You can bring any domain you own, or we'll register one for you (R199/yr for .co.za). We'll point it to our mail servers — free of charge.

Is there a contract?

Nope. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. Annual billing saves you about 15% if you want to lock in a lower rate.

Can I check email on my phone?

Yes! Webmail works in any browser, or you can set up your email in the Gmail app, Apple Mail, Outlook — anything that supports IMAP.

Do you build websites too?

We do. We build fast, secure websites on Netlify — perfect for small businesses who want a professional online presence without WordPress headaches. Ask us for a quote.

How is FUNDA different?

We focus on the fundamentals — done properly. Professional email at your domain, transparent pricing, no upsells, and local support. We keep it simple so you can get on with business.
